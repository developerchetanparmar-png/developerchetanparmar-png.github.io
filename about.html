About CertMock

Last updated: May 1, 2026

What we do

CertMock publishes free, browser-based practice for professional certification exams (for example Microsoft Azure, Microsoft Fabric, and AWS Cloud Practitioner). Each exam has its own page with searchable questions, optional answer reveal, and explanations where available. The goal is to help learners study efficiently and test their knowledge before sitting an official exam.

Independence & trademarks

CertMock is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Microsoft, Amazon Web Services, or other certification vendors. Exam names and codes (such as AZ-900 or CLF-C02) are trademarks of their respective owners and are used here only to identify which skills the practice material targets. See our Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.

How content is built

Questions and explanations are compiled for educational practice. Wording may differ from any specific vendor exam delivery. We do not claim to reproduce proprietary exam items; material is meant as study support, not as a substitute for official curriculum or hands-on experience.
