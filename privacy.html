Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Standard web hosting and content delivery may log technical data (such as IP address, request time, and user agent) as part of normal server or platform operations. That processing is governed by your hosting or CDN provider’s policies.

We may use cookies or similar storage for essential site behavior, analytics, or advertising. You can learn how Google uses data when you use our site in Google’s Partner Sites policy and manage ad personalization in Google Ad Settings.

Third-party services

Pages may load fonts or scripts from third parties (for example Google Fonts or ad networks). Those services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them if you want detail on how they process data.

Children’s privacy

This site is intended for adults and learners preparing for professional certification. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the options on our Contact page (email or GitHub).
